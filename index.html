FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about booking, pricing, and what to expect from EcoClean Pro.

How do I book a cleaning?

Use our online estimate tool to pick your home size and service, then call 1800 789 123 or reach us through the contact section to confirm your date and time.

What’s included in a standard house clean?

Dusting, vacuuming, mopping, kitchen and bathroom sanitising, and general tidying. Deep cleans add intensive work in kitchens, bathrooms, and hard-to-reach areas—tell us your priorities when you book.

Do you bring supplies and equipment?

Yes. Our teams arrive with professional-grade supplies and tools. You can also request eco-friendly products when you schedule your visit.

Are your cleaners insured and background-checked?

All EcoClean Pro staff are vetted, trained, and covered so you can feel comfortable inviting us into your home or office.

What if I need to reschedule or cancel?

Contact us as early as possible. We’ll help you move your booking or cancel according to our policy so you’re never left guessing.

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve Sydney and surrounding suburbs. Ask when you call if you’re on the edge of our coverage area.

Ready to book?

Call us or send a message — we’ll confirm your cleaning in no time.
